About Ting Chen

Graduated from Shih Hsin University with a Bachelor's Degree in Journalism, she is currently studying at the Graduate School of Mass Communication at National Taiwan Normal University.


She has a wide range of research interests, including gender, medical and society, and cross-cultural experiences, etc.


She is interested in public issues, including but not limited to: transitional justice, history, human rights, and education. She is curious and passionate about social science research and is currently exploring it in her graduate studies.
